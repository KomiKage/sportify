Sportify is an app for gyms that let's visitors sign in/out and choose a timeslot.
Sportify helps the staff and visitors safe so everyone can exercise safely.
Use this app to sign in/out when you enter/leave the building.
You can also keep track of your activities and progress to help you stay motivated.

Benefits to using Sportify:

-Easy to integrate for gyms.

-Reserve a timeslot in your favored gym with just one click.

-Simply scan the QR code whilst entering the gym to log in.

-Easiy and clearly keep track of your training register.

-Simply scan out when you leave the gym.

-Get your gym online within 24 hours!
